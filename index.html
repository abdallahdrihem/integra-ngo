About US

Integra is a non-governmental organisation which aims to tackle the integration difficulties in the European society and to offer new
integration possibilities. We are a international group of youth citizens. We recognise the complexity and diversity of the world we live
in as a fact and as a resource. We consider integration as a need of every human being and as our personal mission. Integration for us means giving to everyone the possibility to be respected and enhanced for its own personal and cultural diversity. To this purpose, we
do not exclude any tool: our activities aim to develop the sensitivity and interest for cultural differences, to raise awareness about the minorities difficulties in the European society, to promote education and formation.

Our Services

Here at INTEGRA, we recognize the importance of integrating majority and minority
communities to create a better society for everyone. We provide three main services
to accomplish this goal

Comprehensive Education

INTEGRA provides the community with academic and professional lectures on relevant issues in integration, such as the refugee crisis, migrant policy, and cultural ethics.

Cultural Awareness

INTEGRA provides both minority and majority communities with a window into the others cultures. By raising awareness of cultural similarities and differences, the groundwork for integration can be laid.

Collaborative Action

INTEGRA provides a network for people to better understand how they can assist in social issues. Whether it be with other related NGOs, refugee groups, or citizen platforms, collaboration is a part of everything we do.

Conceptualized Entertainment

INTEGRA provides an inviting environment for personal growth. From
live music to talk shows, from art exhibitions to cultural receptions, we are
devoted to supplying allof our services in ways that everyone can enjoy.

Mission

Give or cause to give equal opportunity and consideration to a racial, religious, or ethnic
group or a member of such a group. Tackle difficulties of integration faced by minorities in
Europe. Spread sensitivity and interest for cultural differences. Promote education and
formation. Raise awareness and offer alternative solutions to the current refugee crisis.

Learn More About Our Event

INTEGRA - Dream of Normal Life

We created this campaign to help fund "Dream of Normal Life," our upcoming event for World Refugee Day focused on understanding the current difficulties of living as a refugee, the ways in which these difficulties can be overcome, and the benefits that are reaped from mutual integration of refugees and their respective new societies. This first event will mark the deployment phase of our NGO, after which point INTEGRA will officially complete its launch as an NGO in Belgium, thus it is important that the event is as successful as possible so we can transition into international cooperation with a good reputation.

INTEGRA - Dream of Normal Life

We know that the situation with the refugees can be frustrating to deal with, most of all for the refugees themselves, as it is largely a legal issue, however we are doing our best to contribute to creating a better world, A world where everyone can live comfortably in an integrated society.

INTEGRA - Dream of Normal Life

Your contribution will allows us to reach out farther into our immediate community with our remembrance service, journey museum, and art exhibition. Each donation will help us to intermediate with better resources the connection between refugees and locals, building bridges to overcome the barriers of intolerance.
Thank you for your support!
