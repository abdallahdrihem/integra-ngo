Dream of Norml Life

Leuven, Martelaarenplein, June 20 - 2016

All Journey Flow Integra Talks Refugees Welcome You in | INTEGRA

in | INTEGRA - Event | Dream of Norml Life

Dream of Normal Life is our World Refugee Day event in Leuven, Belgium. Through this two part event, we are hoping to shine some light on the current situation of refugees and the refugee crisis in Belgium and Europe. The first part from 10:00 to 16:00 is the supporting event focused on displaying the diversity and depth of refugees cultures, and draw similarities between our communities. The second part is our INTEGRA Talks lecture series from 16:00 till 19:00. We will be partnering with academic lecturers, other NGO professionals, and local refugees to provide a fully educational talk show that will only be shown June 20th in front of the Leuven Station. Please join us for this momentous night!
